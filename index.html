THE BUREAU OF PARANORMAL INVESTIGATION

The Bureau of Paranormal Investigation’s mission is to find and catalogue any events, creatures, or items that are outside of the common norm in order to protect humanity. We do this via the following tenets: Find, Research, Secure.

Find

It is our goal to find every single paranormal occurrence within our realm. Because of this we are on the constant look-out for these occurrences and investigate any potential lead to prevent the mistracing of strange events.

Research

The goal is to not simply locate paranormal situations but to understand them. In that manner we strive to make sure that we research every single occurrence. While some are able to be understood, others continue to require more research.

Secure

The most important part is the securing of any physical paranormal manifestations. While some are events that cannot be easily contained, those who are get taken into custody under our jurisdiction. The public should have minimal contact with the paranormal.
