EV-56-████

Status: Ongoing

Event Summary

Orginially appearing in historicla records around ████BC, this entity is linked to numerous unexplained disappearences. The disappearences can range from single individuals to as large as entire settlements and, on one notable occasion, the entirity of the city of ██████, Ohio. While the inexplicable nature of such disappearences makes an accurate count impossible, the entity has been definitively linked to ███████ distinct events. While capture of the entity is considered to be of high priority, agents who encounter the entity are advised to not approach and to contact their nearest field office for backup. In the event of an encounter, protocol C-97 should be enacted to enable proper continuity of Investigation in the event of an agent disappearence.
